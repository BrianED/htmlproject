Frequently Asked Questions



    How much notice should I give when hiring a bouncy castle?

  • Please give at least 2 days notice before your planned event so that we can organize everything for you and to prevent any disappointment



    When will my bouncy castle arrive?

  • Bouncy castles will arrive the night before the booking date!



    Do I have enough space for a bouncy castle in my backyard?

  • Units vary in size but we can arrange for a site visit to find the most suitable!



    How long will it take to blow up the bouncy castle?

  • Not long. It will take about 15 minutes to set up the unit and about 15 minutes for it to be fully inflated. Your ego may take longer!



    Will it send my electricity bill through the roof?

  • It will cost about €2 a day as the fan needs to be kept on to keep the unit inflated!


    What does the price include?

  • The hiring price includes the delivery, collection and setting up the unit!
